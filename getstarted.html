How to get started

Unlike many outdoor sports, getting started with bouldering is surprisingly simple. All you need are some climbing shoes, chalk, even rent the equipment for your first sessions. No prior experience or complex gear required!

The simple steps to get started.

  1. Find a bouldering gym near you.
  2. Rent or invest in a pair of climbing shoes.
  3. Learn basic bouldering techniques.
  4. Start with easy rootes and gradually increas difficulty.
  5. Join a bouldering community or club.
